    Bot bindings (admin only)

    Maps Telegram chatIds to crew member webids. When a bound chatId messages the bot, commands dispatch as that webid (role policy, audit log etc. apply). Unbound chatIds get a friendly hint reply with their chatId — admin pastes it here to bind. To find a chatId: have the new member DM the bot, then hit https://api.telegram.org/bot<TOKEN>/getUpdates and look for "chat":{"id":...}.
